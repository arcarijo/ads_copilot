# Business Profile

## Who We Are
**The Loft on Barton** — a boutique event studio and rental venue in Hamilton, Ontario.
We host intimate weddings, corporate off-sites, photo/video shoots, and pop-up events.

## What We Sell
- Hourly and full-day venue rentals ($150/hr, $900/day)
- Wedding packages ($2,500–$6,000)
- Studio rentals for photographers and content creators

## Target Customers
- Engaged couples (25–40) planning intimate weddings within 50km of Hamilton
- Event planners in the Greater Toronto Area
- Photographers / content creators in Hamilton, Burlington, Oakville, Toronto

## Geography
Primary: Hamilton, ON (15km radius). Secondary: GTA (Toronto, Mississauga, Burlington, Oakville).

## Goals & Economics
- Primary conversion: booking-inquiry form submissions on our website
- Target cost per inquiry (CPA): under $35 CAD
- Typical test budget: $100–$1,000 CAD per campaign, 1 week to 1 month
- Preferred placements: Instagram Feed, Stories, Reels

## Brand Voice
Warm, modern, a little playful. Emphasis on natural light, exposed brick, flexible layouts.

## Constraints
- Strict local geography — do NOT let audience expansion target outside Ontario
- No discounting language; sell on quality and uniqueness of the space
